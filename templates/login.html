What is Humanitarian ID?

Humanitarian ID is an authentication service managed by the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (UNOCHA).

The service is provided freely to the humanitarian community. More information is available at about.humanitarian.id and our team can be contacted at info@humanitarian.id
